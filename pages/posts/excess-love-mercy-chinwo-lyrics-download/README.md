## About Mercy Chinwo

**Mercy Chinwo**, Nigerian singer and song writer was born in PortHarcourt, Nigeria. She released her first single in 2015, Testimony, which was produced by **Rotimi Keys**.

Mercy Chinwo became popular after winning the Nigerian Idol in 2012. She was also nominated for best music, original song at the&#xA0;2013 Ghana Movie Awards.
