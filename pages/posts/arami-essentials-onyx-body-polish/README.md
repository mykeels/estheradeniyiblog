## Arami Essentials Onyx Body Polish

### Product Description

This mixture is a combination of the raw African Black soap, rosewater, ash,&#xA0;pure honey, lemon, extra virgin olive oil, and some other herbs. It is 250ML in quantity and with a black to brown color depending on the process of production.

### How to use the Onyx body polish by Arami Essentials

- Using your finger, scoop some of the product into your palm.
- Rub together in between your palms to cause a lather.
- Rub all over your body to wash
- You can also scoop the Onyx body polish a sponge and wash with it.
- You can use this product daily.
- It is suitable for face and hair.

### onyx Body Polish Benefits

- It helps to soften&#xA0;and hydrate the skin leaving your body feeling smooth and supple.
- One of the benefits of African black soap is that it fights acne. This is why Arami Onyx body polish helps to fight acne.
- Even though it does not cause bleaching or skin&#xA0;lightening, it helps to brighten the skin.
